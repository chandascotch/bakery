Our Story

Chanda Scotch Bakery

At Chanda Scotch Bakery, we believe that the finest moments in life are shared over a perfect crust. Rooted in time-honored traditions and a passion for authentic flavors, our bakery is a celebration of artisanal craftsmanship and premium ingredients.

From our signature golden-baked scotch pies to our delicate, buttery pastries, every item is handcrafted daily to ensure a taste of home in every bite.

Our Mission

"To honor the art of traditional baking by delivering honest, handcrafted goods that bring people together."
